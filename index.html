Dua Lipa

Tour 2022

Dua Lipa is an singer-songwriter from London, England. After her previous work as a model she later signed with Warner Bros record label and since then has released mega hits like 'Don't Start Now', 'Levitating' and 'New Rules'. Her self-named album in 2017 has peaked at number three in the UK Album charts, while her single 'New Rules' has broken into the top 10 within the United States. A superstar of the generation, her 2018 hit single 'One Kiss' in collaboration with Calvin Harris had become the longest-running number one single by a female artist in the the UK. A Grammy-award winning artist in 2019 for Best New Artist, her most recent album and second studio album 'Future Nostalgia' has become critically acclaimed - earning her six Grammy nominations.
